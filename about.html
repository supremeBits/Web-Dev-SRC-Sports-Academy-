About the Academy

Mission, Vision & Values

Our mission is to expand university sports participation, promote physical wellness, and support a culture of discipline and teamwork.

We envision SRC SPORT ACADEMY as a leading regional hub for athletes, students, and local residents seeking quality coaching and community sport opportunities.

Values include inclusion, excellence, sustainability, and community-driven growth.

Funding & Support

The academy is funded by university partnerships, sports development grants, and community sponsorship programs.

Support from local sports bodies and alumni networks ensures continued growth and scholarships for talented athletes.

How We Operate

Through structured teams, accessible booking, and coach-led training programs, SRC SPORT ACADEMY provides a modern web platform for sports engagement.
